Welcome to Munneries

We are leading retail and wholesale suppliers of high-class fresh produce, vegetables, salads, fruit, prepared produce and dairy based in the south of England.

Our ethos is to provide exceptional standards of quality and service in all areas of our business.

Our core business is the wholesale supply and distribution of high-class fresh produce and dairy. Our premises comprise a purpose-built unit containing cold storage facilities, air conditioned order preparation/picking area, ambient storage and covered loading bay, complete with a state-of-the-art computer system.

Munneries is also very proud to boast an impressive and extensive retail outlet and floristry department, which serves the local community and beyond.
